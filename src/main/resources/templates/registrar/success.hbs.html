Congratulations! Your blog was created.

What is next?

  1. Go to your blog at {{siteUrl}}. The Moera client will be loaded automatically and Moera logo in the top-left corner of the page will become orange.

    {{image "/pics/registrar/logo.png"}}

  2. Click "Connect" button in the top-right corner of the page.

    {{image "/pics/registrar/connect.gif"}}

  3. In "Connect To Home" dialog, check "Password haven't been set yet" checkbox and enter the password you want to set.

    {{image "/pics/registrar/connect-dialog.gif"}}

  4. Now your blog is under your control.

Creating a Moera name

Moera name is used for communication between Moera users. It is yours regardless of the location of the blog.

  1. Open your PROFILE and click the blue button "Register New".

    {{image "/pics/registrar/register-new.gif"}}

  2. Enter the name you want. It must have at least 4 characters and may contain any letters (upper or lower case, latin or non-latin), digits and some punctuation.

    {{image "/pics/registrar/enter-name.gif"}}

  3. If the name is correct and available, a list of 24 words will be shown. These words are needed to control the name. Save them however you want: make a screenshot, print, write down. The order of the words is important.

    {{image "/pics/registrar/secret-words.png"}}

  4. In a minute the name will appear in your blog.

    {{image "/pics/registrar/name.png"}}

That's all! Now you can create your first post.
